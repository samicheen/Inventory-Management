Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Vinayaka Inventory Management ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Inventory Management mobile application and web services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (username, email address)
  • Business data (inventory items, purchases, sales, manufacturing records)
  • Vendor and customer information
  • Barcode and product information

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Usage data (features used, timestamps)
  • Camera access (for barcode scanning functionality)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our inventory management services
  • Process transactions and manage your inventory
  • Authenticate users and secure access to your account
  • Improve our app functionality and user experience
  • Send important service-related communications

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud

6. Camera and Barcode Scanning

Our app requires camera access to scan barcodes for inventory management. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Barcode information is stored as part of your inventory records.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. To request account deletion:

  1. Contact us via email at yashpalkhariwal@gmail.com or samicheen@gmail.com
  2. Include your username or email address associated with your account
  3. Specify that you wish to delete your account

What will be deleted:

  • Your user account (username, email, password)
  • All inventory data associated with your account
  • All purchase records
  • All sales records
  • All manufacturing records
  • All vendor and customer information

Data Retention: Once your account deletion request is processed, your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. Some data may be retained for legal or accounting purposes as required by law, but will not be accessible through the application.

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please ensure you have exported any data you wish to keep before requesting deletion.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

10. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for business use and is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
